# HaHu: Speak! — Privacy Policy

**Effective: 05/31/2026**

HaHu: Speak! does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app uses your device's microphone to record audio for pronunciation feedback. These recordings are processed entirely on-device and are not sent to any server. The app does not require an account, does not use third-party analytics, and works entirely offline.
